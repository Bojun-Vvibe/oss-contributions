## Summary

Adds a new `STATUS_COLORS` registry under `packages/opencode/src/cli/cmd/tui/context/status-colors.ts` mapping six task states (`running`, `waiting`, `attention`, `error`, `done`, `idle`) to a `{color, bg, icon, text, description}` tuple, plus a `<StatusIndicator>` component, plus reconnecting-state plumbing in the prompt. Updates `event.ts` (adds `projectName` to the toast schema) and `prompt/index.tsx` to wire a 1-second-debounced "reconnecting" indicator next to the existing retry one.

## Verdict: `request-changes`

Three concrete blockers before this can land:

1. **Banned strings ship in the diff.** `status-colors.ts` hard-codes Spanish UI strings (`"Ejecutando..."`, `"Esperando respuesta"`, `"Requiere atención"`, `"Completado"`, `"Inactivo"`) in a `STATUS_COLORS[].text` field that the new `<StatusIndicator>` renders verbatim. opencode's TUI is English-only elsewhere; either route these through the existing i18n surface or keep them English. This is the single biggest blocker.
2. **Lockfile drift.** `bun.lock:2152` removes the integrity hash (`"sha512-…"`) from `@solidjs/start@https://pkg.pr.new/...` while leaving every neighbouring lock entry hashed. That's a genuine correctness regression — without the hash, `bun install` will accept any tarball at that pkg.pr.new URL. Either restore the integrity field or re-pin to a versioned semver dep.
3. **+4668 / −5004 is far too large for a "color convention + icons" feature.** The diff body advertises a contained UI change but the actual diff also rewrites the prompt component (`prompt/index.tsx:1369-1404` adds a whole new reconnecting-banner subtree with its own `createSignal`/`createEffect`/`onCleanup` machinery), changes the toast schema (`event.ts:38` adds `projectName` to `Schema.Struct`), and touches enough other files to net out −336 lines. Split into at least three PRs: (a) `status-colors.ts` + `<StatusIndicator>` only; (b) the reconnecting indicator; (c) the toast `projectName` field — each can be reviewed on its own.
